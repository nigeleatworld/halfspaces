04 / Introspace

Trust-based referrals as a workflow.

2025/Idea/concept

I wanted to make the invisible work around referrals more explicit: context, trust, fit, and the right to say no.

What it is

A private professional network for people in transition and connectors willing to make trust-based referrals.

Why I built it

Cold applications flatten people. Good introductions carry context, but most tools do not structure that context well.
